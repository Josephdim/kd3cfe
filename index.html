Introduction

An antenna is a device that converts electrical signals into radio waves and vice versa. They are used in radios, TVs, Wi-Fi, and ham radio setups. Proper antenna design is crucial for effective communication.

Types of Antennas

  • Dipole Antenna: Simple and widely used for radio frequencies.
  • Yagi Antenna: Directional antenna used for long-range communication.
  • Vertical Antenna: Great for omnidirectional coverage.
  • Loop Antenna: Compact design, often used for shortwave bands.
