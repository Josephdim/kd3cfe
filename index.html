All About Antennas

Introduction

An antenna is a device that converts electrical signals into radio waves and vice versa. They are used in radios, TVs, Wi-Fi, and ham radio setups.

Types of Antennas

  • Dipole Antenna
  • Yagi Antenna
  • Vertical Antenna
  • Loop Antenna
